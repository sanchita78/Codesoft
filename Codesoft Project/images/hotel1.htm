    TravelX is my first full-scale website project, designed to provide users with a seamless travel booking experience. From selecting destinations to booking hotels and flights, the platform offers an intuitive and visually appealing interface. This project helped me improve my UI/UX design skills, understand industry standards, and create a structured booking flow.

    The TravelX Home Page is designed to give users a smooth start to their travel booking experience. It features:

    ✅ Hero Section: A visually appealing banner with an engaging headline and search functionality for destinations.

    ✅ Navigation Bar: Includes Home, Destinations, Hotels, Flights, Help, About Us, Blog, and a search function. The profile icon and language selection options provide personalization.

    ✅ Popular Destinations Section: Showcases trending travel locations with eye-catching images and starting prices.

    ✅ Featured Hotels Section: Highlights top-rated hotels with quick booking options.

    ✅ User-Friendly Design: The page layout ensures smooth scrolling and clear call-to-action buttons, making navigation easy.

    The Hotel Details Page provides users with all necessary information about a selected hotel, ensuring an informed booking decision. Key elements include:

    ✅ High-Quality Images: Showcases the hotel’s exterior, rooms, and amenities.

    ✅ Hotel Description: A short but detailed overview of the hotel's atmosphere, style, and location.

    ✅ Room Categories & Pricing: Displays different room options with prices (starting from $1,000 in your project).

    ✅ Key Facilities: Lists important features such as Wi-Fi, breakfast, pool, and room service.

    ✅ Booking Button: A clear CTA for users to proceed with their reservation.

    ✅ Sticky Navigation Bar: Ensures users can switch between pages without hassle.
